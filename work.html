Projects

Future For Sale

Future For Sale explores how everyday products reveal larger environmental issues connected to waste, fossil fuels, and consumer culture. Through conceptual design and visual storytelling, the project encourages viewers to reflect on the long-term consequences of modern consumption.

Dream Then Rebuild

This typography project focuses on themes of rebuilding and resilience using structured, layered design elements.

Bloom

Bloom reflects life after experiencing death and grief. The piece represents healing, growth, and becoming myself again after loss.

Ocean Conservation

Ocean Conservation explores the relationship between people and marine ecosystems through photography, typography, and environmental storytelling. The project highlights the importance of protecting ocean habitats and preserving biodiversity.

After Consumption

After Consumption examines what remains after products are used and discarded. Through visual communication and environmental themes, the project encourages reflection on waste, overconsumption, and the lasting effects of human behavior on the environment.

Portrait Painting

This project explores realism and emotional expression through portrait painting techniques, careful observation, and personal storytelling.
